torrentio

10 min read feb 2026

overview

torrentio is the most popular stremio addon. it fetches torrents from various sources and allows you to stream them directly within stremio.

before you begin

you may need a debrid service depending on the country you live in, it is also generally recommended for stable streams that don't require torrenting.

follow the debrid guide to get started with debrid services.

note: all (good) debrid services cost money.

configuration steps

1

navigate to the torrentio configuration page.

2

optional step - select your debrid provider and enter your API key.

3

configure quality filters and sorting options.

for example if your device is only capable of 1080p, exclude the 4k option in the quality filter.

4

install the configured addon to stremio.

settings overview

torrentio exposes a variety of options in its settings panel. below is a brief description of each setting.

providers

select the torrent providers you want to use, these are like a list of sources that torrentio will search through. it is best to leave all providers enabled to maximize the number of available streams.

sorting

choose how torrentio should sort results. depending on your setup (debrid or no debrid) this will differ.

sorting by quality then seeders will prioritize higher quality torrents, then the number of seeders.

sorting by quality then size will prioritize higher quality torrents, then the size of the torrent (smaller torrents are generally seeded more and will work better on a slow connection, use this option carefully).

sorting by seeders will prioritize torrents with more seeders (more people sharing the torrent). this is best for a configuration without a debrid service that "just works"

sorting by size will prioritize larger torrents, which are generally higher quality but less seeded and may be slower to start or buffer during playback on slower connections.

seeders: seeders are the number of people sharing the torrent, more seeders generally means a more stable stream.

note: if you use a debrid service, sorting by seeders is irrelevant as the debrid provider likely has the highest quality stream cached already

max results per quality

the maximum number of results torrentio will return for each quality tier.

priority language

if you wish to see english results you do not need to add a priority language. this is purely for people who are not fluent in english and want to prioritize streams that have their native language as an audio track.

exclude resolutions

exclude specific resolutions from the results.

bluray remux - enable if you are on a slow connection, as these files are often 50gb+.

hdr/hdr10+/dolby vision related settings - you likely know if your tv is capable of these qualities. if not, check your tv's specifications.

3d - you most likely want to enable this unless you have a 3d capable device such as a VR headset.

non 3d - select this to only show 3d results. recommended for people with 3d capable setups or the stremio meta quest app.

4k, 1080p, 720p, 480p - select "4k" to exclude 4k content, "1080p" to exclude 1080p content, etc.

other (dvdrip, hdrip, bdrip) - enable to remove torrents ripped from blu-ray, dvd and hdtv sources (useful for only streaming-app level quality or remux)

cam/screener - enable if you don't want to see low quality torrents. these are often somebody recording the movie in a cinema with poor audio and video quality.

unknown - not recommended to enable as niche content without a proper resolution tag may be excluded from results.

video size limit

directly from the torrentio page: 'returned videos cannot exceed this size, use comma to have different size for movies and series. examples: 5GB ; 800MB ; 10GB,2GB'

debrid provider

select the debrid service you wish to use (real-debrid, torbox, etc.) or select "none" if you do not use one. a debrid service will be more stable as it doesn't require you to download via torrenting.

for more information on debrid services, see the debrid guide.

api key

enter your debrid account api key. this field is required when a provider is selected; otherwise torrentio will be unable to use debrid services.

don't show download to debrid links

this will show cached results only. this is hit or miss depending on your debrid provider's api (real-debrid no longer has a reliable way to see if a result is cached).

dont show debrid catalog

the debrid catalog can be found at the bottom of your stremio homepage, and will display a list of all torrents that have been downloaded from your debrid service recently. this is not stremio-account specific.

if you encounter issues with specific settings, retrace your steps above, make a reddit post on r/StremioAddons or contact me on discord and i'll attempt to help (no promises).
