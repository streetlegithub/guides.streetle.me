debrid guide

12 min read feb 2026

overview

a debrid service acts as a middleman between you and torrent sources. instead of downloading torrents directly (which can be slow and legally risky), a debrid service downloads the content to their really fast servers first, then streams it to you over a direct HTTPS connection (much harder to detect illegal streaming).

this means faster speeds, no need to seed (and less of an effect on the torrenting scene as a whole), and your ISP only sees HTTPS encrypted traffic to the debrid server - not the content you're actually watching or any metadata associated with it.

notes (please read): all debrid services listed here cost money. there are no good 'free' debrid services

prices may not be up to date - always check the official site for current pricing.

i show the cheapest price available, this is usually through a subscription plan rather than a one-time purchase.

i have not tested all of the services on this list apart from real-debrid and put.io. i will do a full audit on each service if i recieve enough donations to justify the time investment. in the meantime, the pros/cons sections are based on my research and user feedback from various online communities (usually reddit obviously), but take them with a grain of salt as i have not personally verified all of the claims made about each service.

which one should i pick?

most stremio users start with real-debrid or torbox. real-debrid is the most popular and cheapest option, while torbox has the best stremio integration, active development and a good support team who responds. see the pros/cons for each service below to decide.
